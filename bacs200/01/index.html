About Sean

One of the most notable things about me is my love of travel. I believe that when you get the chace to travel to a place that you’ve never been before, it’s a chance to make new memories and have experinces that not everyone gets to, and it’s important to live life to the fullest. I have had the opportunity to explore the Galapagos Islands, a small set of islands about 300 miles west of Ecuador, which are home to some of the most unique and exquisit wildlife on the planet. I was able to get within feet of sea lions, sea turtles, penguins, and the famous Galapagos Tortoise. I learned a lot from the trip, and plan to make many more memories in many more amazing places.
