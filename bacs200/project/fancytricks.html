Golf is one of the most interesting games to play. It is more of a mental game than a physical game. Many people aren't too fond of golf, but if you really have a passion for it, it is a game that you will play for the rest of your life. Golf is a game where the only way you lose, is when you put your clubs down. If you break a hundred or two hundred, that just means that there is room for improvement. Golf is something that I will play for the rest of my life.

The more I learn about software engineering and how it works, the more I realize that software is everywhere in today's world. There is software in the newest cars and trucks, houses, and even something as simple as a crosswalk. The moment that you push that button, a whole connection of software is set off and recognizes that someone pushed that button to cross the street. It is amazing how software is everywhere. I have even heard professionals speak about software and they said that almost every single company today, is a software comapny. It does not matter what product or industry they are in, they are a software company.

One of my goals in my life is to travel to a lot of different countries. The summer of 2017, I took the most amazing trip that I have ever been on to the Galapagos Islands off the coast of Ecuador. It was a science school trip and it really opened my eyes to places other than the United States. I saw some of the most amazing wildife that I have ever seen, had some of the best food, and saw how other cultures lived their day to day lives. I want to expand my mind even more and travel to places such as Australia, Ireland, Tanzania, and many more. I belive that if you don't travel in your life, then you don't really have any idea as to what is out there in the world.
