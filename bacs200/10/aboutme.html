About Me

Sean Strawmatt is a sophmore at the University of Northern Colorado. He is currently pursuing a bachelors degree in software engineering. He continues to learn new concepts every day from a multitude of different programming langueages such as Python, Java, C#, and currently HTML and CSS. Sean is the first in his family to attend a university with a four year degree. He makes his family proud by trying his best and working extremely hard. Other than school related things, Sean lives his life to the fullest. When not in class, Sean is often outside running, doing some sort of physical labor, or working on his golf swing. Last summer, Sean scored an awesome job as a maintenance crew member at a golf course near his house in Aurora. He has often said that the golf course is the best job that he has ever had because he developed a deeper love for the game, as well as a deeper understanding of gold course matinence and the outdoors. Sean started work at 5 AM, but Sean said that getting up early is well worth the beautiful morning view.

Hobbies
